Engineering Principles

Building better tools through technical discipline.

01. Native Performance

Unlike hybrid apps built with web-wrappers, every app in this collection is built using 100% native Kotlin and Jetpack Compose. This ensures zero "jank" and the absolute minimum battery impact on your Android device. We prioritize performance as a core feature, not an afterthought.

02. Data Sovereignty

Privacy is the default state. Most apps in the Simply Collection™ operate with a local-first architecture. This means your personal data, logs, and high scores never leave your physical device unless you explicitly choose to export them. No cloud accounts, no shadow-tracking, no surveillance.
