Engineering Principles

Building better tools through technical discipline.

01. Native Performance

Unlike hybrid apps built with web-wrappers, every app in this collection is built using 100% native Kotlin and Jetpack Compose. This ensures zero "jank" and the absolute minimum battery impact on your Android device.

02. Data Sovereignty

Privacy is the default state. Most apps in the Simply Collection™ operate with a local-first architecture. Your personal data and logs never leave your physical device unless you explicitly choose to export them.

Fast & Focused

No bloat. No long loading screens. Every Simply app is designed to let you do what you need and get on with your day.

Private by Design

Your data belongs to you. Simply apps keep your logs and habits strictly on your own device.

Modern Interface

Built using Jetpack Compose for a butter-smooth experience that feels at home on any modern Android device.
