Child Safety Standards

Protecting the most vulnerable users through architectural integrity and strict policy enforcement.

Zero Tolerance Policy

Simply Connect™ maintains a zero-tolerance policy regarding Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) and Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE). We are committed to providing a platform that prioritizes user safety and complies with all legal requirements for child protection.

Text-Only Architecture

To fundamentally eliminate the risk of inappropriate visual content, Simply Connect™ is designed strictly for text-based communication. The ability to send or receive pictures, videos, and other media files is disabled at the source code level. There is no workaround, ensuring a text-only environment for all family users.

Ephemeral Data Cycles

By design, all communication within "Grid Rooms" is automatically deleted from our servers within 24 hours. This transitory environment ensures that data is not stored long-term, minimizing the digital footprint of our minor users and preventing long-term exposure to content.

Reporting & Legal Compliance

We actively cooperate with regional and national authorities, including NCMEC (National Center for Missing & Exploited Children), to report and combat illegal activity. Users are encouraged to report violations directly to the developer via the Contact Page.
