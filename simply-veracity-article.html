Simply Veracity™

Secured trust, made simple.

The Backstory

Trust isn't just a requirement in a relationship; it’s the foundation. After years of building software, I realized that the best tools we use in our daily lives—the ones we truly rely on—don't need to track our every move to be effective.

The Privacy Gap

When I look at the state of digital tools, there’s an unnecessary barrier: the demand for your data. The model used by giant corporations is built on the assumption that for a tool to be useful, it must be invasive. I believe that is a fundamental misunderstanding of what a tool should be.

Engineering Integrity

I built Simply Veracity™ to challenge that model. It’s an engine designed for high-fidelity verification and automated quality control, but with one critical difference: we keep nothing.

It is built on a local-first architecture because I believe that technical integrity shouldn't come at the cost of your digital sovereignty. Your data belongs to you, and your logs stay on your device.

The Mission

My goal has always been to build clean, functional software my girls can be proud of. That means building things that are useful, fast, and—most importantly—built to respect the user, not exploit them.
