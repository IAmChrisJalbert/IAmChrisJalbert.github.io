HI, I'M CHRIS

I am an Android Developer based out of Gloucester, Massachusetts. By day, I navigate the fast-paced and complex world of freight forwarding. By night, I build clean, fast, and private mobile applications for Android.

I started the "Simply" Collection because I was tired of mobile apps filled with bloated menus, intrusive tracking, and unnecessary data hoarding. I wanted tools and games that just work—apps designed specifically to respect your time, battery life, and privacy.

When I'm not writing Kotlin code, designing user interfaces, or managing freight logistics, I'm usually spending my time with my incredible wife, Amber, and my two awesome kids, Ace and Emersyn.
