Data Sovereignty Standards

Privacy Policy

The Simply Collection™ Standard

Apps including Simply Sip™, Simply Minesweeper™, and Simply Solitaire™ are built on a Local-First architecture. Your personal data (logs, high scores, and settings) is stored exclusively on your device. We do not transmit or store this data on our servers.

Service Providers

To support the development and functionality of our apps, we use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: Used for banner advertisements. Google may collect device identifiers and location data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.
  • Google Firebase: Used specifically for Simply Connect™ to facilitate real-time messaging and temporary data transit.

Note: You can manage your ad personalization settings directly through your Android device's Google Settings.

Simply Connect™

1. Data Minimization

Simply Connect™ does not require account registration. We do not collect phone numbers, emails, or contacts. Your "Display Name" and "Room Code" are strictly for identifying your presence within a temporary session.

2. 24-Hour Ephemeral Purge

Messages sent through Simply Connect™ are stored in the Firebase Realtime Database for transit purposes only. All active message data is automatically purged from the database within 24 hours. We do not maintain historical archives or permanent logs of user conversations.

3. Children's Privacy

In accordance with our Child Safety Standards, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Simply Connect™ is designed as a text-only, media-free environment to ensure a secure space for family communication.
