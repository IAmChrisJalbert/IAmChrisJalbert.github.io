Simply Connect™

Secure. Ephemeral. Family. Re-imagining messaging as a transitory tool.

Simply Connect™ was born from a simple question: "Why does our data need to live forever?" For family conversations, simple logistics, or private check-ins, the modern chat archive is a liability. Simply Connect™ solves this by applying a strict 24-hour expiration policy to every byte of data generated.

The "Janitor" Architecture

Using Firebase Realtime Database, we implemented a server-side "Janitor" script. Every message is timestamped upon creation. Our logic engine continuously scans for any data exceeding the 24-hour mark and wipes it from the active nodes. This means your communication is transitory by nature—it exists only as long as it is useful.

Anonymity by Design

Simply Connect™ does not require an email, phone number, or social media link. We utilize Firebase Anonymous Authentication to generate a secure session tied strictly to your hardware. Users communicate using "Secret Room Codes," which act as the private gateways for family or project groups. This removes the barrier to entry while maximizing the privacy of the participants.

"We don't want your life story. We just want to give you a private space to talk today, and a clean slate to start tomorrow."

Safety and Ethics

In adherence to our strict Child Safety Standards, Simply Connect™ is architected as a text-only platform. By disabling media uploads at the source-code level, we eliminate the primary vector for inappropriate content exchange, making it a safe, efficient environment for families and professional teams alike.
