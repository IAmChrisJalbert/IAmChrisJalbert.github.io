Simply Connect™

Secure. Ephemeral. Family. Re-imagining messaging as a transitory tool for the modern age.

The Problem: Data Permanence

Simply Connect™ was born from a simple question: "Why does our data need to live forever?" For family logistics or private check-ins, the permanent chat archive is a liability. We solve this with a strict 24-hour expiration policy applied to every byte of data generated.

The "Janitor" Architecture

Utilizing the Firebase Realtime Database, we implemented a server-side "Janitor" script. Every message is timestamped upon creation. Our logic engine continuously scans and wipes data exceeding the 24-hour mark. This ensures communication is transitory—it exists only as long as it is useful.

  • • Firebase Anonymous Auth
  • • Realtime Data Purge
  • • Secret Room Gateways
  • • Text-Only Architecture

Anonymity by Design

Simply Connect™ requires no email or phone number. We utilize Firebase Anonymous Authentication to tie sessions strictly to hardware. Access is managed via "Secret Room Codes," acting as private gateways for family or project groups—maximizing privacy while removing barriers to entry.

"We don't want your life story. We just want to give you a private space to talk today, and a clean slate to start tomorrow."

Safety & Compliance

Protecting the digital footprint of our users is our highest priority. Simply Connect™ is architected as a media-free environment, disabling pictures and videos at the source code level to maintain a secure communication space.
