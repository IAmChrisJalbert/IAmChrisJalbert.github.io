DAVE RUSSO: THE ENTERTAINER

From the wrestling mats of Massachusetts to the bright lights of Las Vegas, Dave Russo has mastered the art of the crowd. A former collegiate athlete, Dave swapped the mat for the spotlight, landing national commercials before finding his true calling in stand-up comedy.

His breakout moment came winning the inaugural Boston Comedy Festival, which launched him onto the national stage. He was the only comedian to reach the finals of Wayne Newton’s The Entertainer on E!, securing a Las Vegas headlining contract.

CAREER HIGHLIGHTS

Beyond his national touring, Dave is a familiar face on New England television, notably serving as a host for the hit show Dirty Water TV and making appearances on The Today Show.

COMMUNITY & PHILANTHROPY

Dave is deeply committed to giving back, frequently collaborating with the Neely House and the Celebrities for Charity Foundation to support community initiatives and scholarship programs throughout Massachusetts.
